Ingredients:
 - 1 egg
 - bit of butter

Directions:
 1. Take a frying pan
 2. Heat it up
 3. When hot, add some butter
 4. When butter is melted, break the egg and let it carefully out on the frying pan
 5. Turn down the heat to medium warm
 6. Wait until the egg is almost dry
 7. Then flip the egg
 8. Give it about 20 seconds on that other side as well
 9. Done!
